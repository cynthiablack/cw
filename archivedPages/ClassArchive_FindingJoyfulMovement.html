Finding Joyful Movement

4-Week Virtual Series

Donation Based and/or Free

Through trauma informed, equitable, accessible, community based and healing Yoga practice and Meditations.

Class Outcomes

  • Experience movement that feels good in your body.
  • Try out different Yoga poses with both chair and mat practice options.
  • Experience movement using props found in your home, or more traditional yoga props.
  • Learn basic asana names (such as easy seat, sun salutation, warrior I and II, eagle, pigeon, etc.)
  • Build knowledge of class format (settle-in, heating the body, balancing the body, meditation/letting movement integrate into your mind and body.)
  • Mindful practices you can use on your own or in your community.

Featuring possible special guest appearance by our new pup, Winnie!
