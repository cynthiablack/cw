About Me

Hi, I'm Christy! I am a 200 RYT Certified Yoga Instructor. I am trained through Breath For Change that intentionally aims to use trauma-informed, equitable, accessible, community based and a healing approach to Yoga and mindfulness breathing techniques. I am a Teacher and a Student working to co-create, with you, joyful movement for all bodies and ANYBODY.

Will you join me?
